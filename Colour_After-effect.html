Colour After-effect



This page lets you demonstrate the "colour after-effect" on yourself.  More than just being an interesting experiment, the colour after-effect enables you to deduce the that you have 3 cone types, and to work out the colours they are most sensitive to.

The procedure is as follows.
1.   Sit a comfortable distance from the screen.
2.   Stare at the cross in the middle of the coloured squares ("fixate" on the cross).  It is important not to let your gaze drift because you want to expose a fixed group of your cones to each colour to allow their photo-pigment to bleach.
3.   Count slowly to 30.
4.   Switch your fixation point to the cross on the right-hand side of the screen and record the colours that you see.  Try hard blinking to intensify the colours.

There are 3 sets of squares to test.  Just follow the link on each page after you have recorded the colours you saw.
